risotto

ingredients

3 cups of rice

1 chopped onion

1 liter and half of water

4 soup spoons of butter

Seasoning of your preference

The chosen cheese

Modo de preparo

Saute the onion with two spoons of butter then put the cups of rice, wait a little
and saute again with your seasoning of preference. Slowly put in the water, and
put the rest of the butter with your cheese. Wait until the butter melts and mix
slowly, It's ready!
