Soufflé

ingredients

3 tablespoons unsalted butter

1/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons of the chosen cheese

3 tablespoons flour

1 1/4 cups heavy cream

4 large eggs, separated, plus 3 large egg whites

3 tablespoons dry sherry

2 tablespoons sour cream

1 1/4 teaspoons salt

1 teaspoon Dijon mustard

1/2 teaspoon dry mustard

1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper

1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar

Modo de preparo

First preheat the oven to 180 degrees. Butter a 1 1/2-quart soufflé dish and coat it
with 2 tablespoons of the chosen cheese. In a medium saucepan over medium-high
heat, melt butter. Stir in flour to make a paste. Gradually whisk in cream and bring
mixture to a boil over moderate heat, whisking constantly. Reduce the heat to low
and cook, whisking, until very thick, 3 minutes. Transfer base to a large bowl; let
cool. Stir in egg yolks, sherry, the chosen cheese, sour cream, salt, Dijon mustard,
dry mustard, and cayenne. Put egg whites in a large stainless steel bowl. Add
cream of tartar. Using an electric mixer, beat egg whites until firm peaks form.
Fold one-third of the whites into the soufflé base to lighten it, then fold in
remaining whites until no streaks remain. Scrape mixture into prepared dish. Run
your thumb or a towel around the inside rim of the dish to wipe away any crumbs.
Bake for about 35 minutes, until the soufflé is golden brown and puffed. Serve
right away.
