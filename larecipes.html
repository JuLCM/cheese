Lasagna

ingredients

12 to 15 lasagna noodles, preferably no-boil noodles

8 ounces whole milk ricotta cheese and mascarpone cheese

1 large egg, lightly beaten

1 cup (2 ounces) grated Parmigiano Reggiano
or Grana Padano cheese

1/4 teaspoon fine sea salt and fresh ground black pepper

1/2 cup fresh basil leaves, torn into small pieces

8 ounces mozzarella, shredded, 2 packed cups

5 ounces fontina cheese, thinly sliced or shredded

one 28-ounce jar of spaghetti sauce

Modo de preparo

Start heading the oven to 180 degrees. Mix the ricotta, mascarpone, parmesan, salt, pepper,
and beaten egg in a medium bowl and spread one cup of sauce over the bottom of the dish.
Place 3 to 4 lasagna noodles down, overlapping them slightly.Spread half of the
ricotta-mascarpone mixture over the noodles, then scatter half of the basil leaves on top. Add
a second layer of noodles, and then spread 1 cup of sauce over them. Scatter half of the
mozzarella cheese and half of the fontina cheese over the sauce. Add a third layer of noodles,
spread the remaining ricotta-mascarpone mixture over them, and then scatter the remaining
basil leaves on top. Finish with the fourth layer of noodles and spread the remaining sauce on
top. Bake the lasagna uncovered until the cheese bubbles and the noodles cook; about 30 minutes.
