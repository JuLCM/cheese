Morro Azul

Morro Azul is wrapped in a wooden belt (oak), inspired by the Swiss cheese Vacherin Mont
dâ€™Or. Morro Azul was created here in Pomerode and its name pays homage to one of the
city's symbols. After going through a production process aimed at retaining moisture,
always with the aim of giving the cheese high creaminess, it is salted and
subsequently wrapped in a wooden ring, which guarantees structure and helps with the
composition of the aroma and flavor.

Morro Azul is a very smooth and very creamy cheese. Buttery and dairy notes are
predominant. Good to have it with friends for your classic but delicate flavor.
