Lua Cheia

Lua Cheia is one of the most famous cheeses from the Serra das Antas cheese factory,
which is why it has already received several awards over the years. Made with
pasteurized cow's milk and aged for 3 months, Lua Cheia has a higher fat
content, the result of the double addition of cream to the dough.A thin layer of edible
charcoal is added to its flowery shell, and the texture is extremely creamy. One of those
cheeses that melt in your mouth !

When you taste it, you can expect a smooth, slightly sweet and buttery flavor.
Enjoy it with delicious honey, fruit jelly and dried fruits.
It pairs very well with light white wines. On a charcuterie board,
combine it with blue and spicier cheeses.
