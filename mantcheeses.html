Queijo Azul da Mantiqueira

Queijo Azul da Mantiqueira was born in honor of the drovers who for decades pushed
production from the mountain range to the lower lands.Inspired by its region of origin,
this special cheese combined the flavor that follows the European standard with
the Minas Gerais tradition that passes from generation to generation.It uses cream
in the production, adding more creaminess to the cheese.

It has a slightly salty flavor, creamy dough and blue mold present in harmony.
It's not extremely strong but very tasty.
