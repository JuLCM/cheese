History

When the ancient people started to domesticate animals the opportunity to consume milk
appeared but with this new opportunity new challenges also appeared.


They need to find a way to preserve the milk so the cheese was created. Over the years
cheese became a really sophisticated food that people would pay amounts of money to try,
and one day it came to Brazil.


In Brazil cheese became a really popular food and we can find different kinds in many
brazilian dishes all over the country, but even though cheeses are really popular we have
some regions where its more than just cheese, is tradition.


In Minas Gerais cheese became a really important part of the local culture and nowadays
people come from all over the country to try the most exotic and delicious
cheese that they have.
