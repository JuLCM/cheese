Fondue

ingredients

1 garlic clove, halved

1 cup of he chosen cheese

1 cup dry white wine

1 tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon cornstarch

1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice

Freshly ground black pepper

Freshly grated nutmeg

Modo de preparo

To start, rub the inside of a cheese fondue pot with the garlic clove; discard the
garlic. Combine your chosen cheese with the wine, cornstarch, and lemon juice in
the fondue pot and cook over moderate heat, stirring occasionally, until the cheese
begins to melt, about 5 minutes. Reduce heat to low. Add a generous pinch of
pepper and nutmeg and cook, stirring gently, until creamy and smooth, about 3
minutes; don't overcook the fondue or it will get stringy. Serve at once.
