tulha

Queijo Tulha gained fame with its awards in Brazil in 2016 and 2017, and for being the first
Brazilian artisanal cheese to win an International gold medal, a title achieved at the
World Cheese Awards in San Sebastian, Spain.

Produced at Fazenda Atalaia, located in the city of Amparo - SÃ£o Paulo. Tulha is a cheese
aged for 12 months, made with type A Dutch cow's milk. It has a crumbly dough with
crystals that melt in the mouth.

This type of production is what provides the reddish skin for the delicacy.
This is a cheese that pairs perfectly with cheese and charcuterie boards, and red wine.
Shortcrust pastry with crystals and a taste with fruity notes. Flavor reminiscent of salted
caramel. Unique !
